Welcome to the Faculty Portal of IIITB

Via the Faculty Portal – all information pertaining to the classes and students you teach can be submitted and updated. A Faculty can view their student’s Subject Registrations; Attendance and Academic Report Cards via their Portal. The Faculty can upload work/topics that have been covered in class and documents for the topic can also be downloaded via the Parent/Guardian and Student Portals e.g.; for Units of Inquiry by subject; rubrics etc. A Faculty can upload their student’s homework assignments/projects and any documents for their class/or assign as group of students/or as individual students and these can be downloaded by Parent/Guardian and Student Portals. Faculty can also view their students Code of Conduct and positive and negative incidents. Faculty can also view their students houses and Extra-Curriculum activities and they can also send email and SMS notifications to Parents/Guardians and Students.
